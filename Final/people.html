Daoyun Ji

I go by Ji, a last-name turned nickname. I hope you feel comfortable to call me just that.

 

Undergraduate, graduate students and postdocs are needed to join me at Baylor College of Medicine. Anyone who are interested, please contact Ji at dji@mit.edu.

Students and scientists with interests in cellular, systems, behavioral, and/or computational aspects of learning and memory are welcome. Experimental works include recordings in freely moving rats and mice, and in brain slices. Theoretical works include analyzing realistic electrophysiological data and theoretical models. The goal is to build a diverse group with backgrounds in areas such as cell biology, neurobiology, physics, engineering, or mathematics. Those with strong theoretical and engineering training may find it fascinating to apply their skills in important neuroscience problems, such as how memories are formed and why we dream.
